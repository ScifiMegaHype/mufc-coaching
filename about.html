Our Mission

Since Sir Alex retired, supporting United has been an emotional rollercoaster. Our mission is simple: to help fans manage their expectations, avoid heartbreak, and find joy even in tough times.

Our Approach

Our certified psychologists use humor, community, and practical coaching techniques to turn crushing defeats into teachable moments. We’re not miracle workers — but we can help you manage dissapointments, control unrealistic expectations and stay grounded during CL qualification delusion episodes.
